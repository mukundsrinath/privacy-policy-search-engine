About Us

PrivaSeer is an evolving privacy policy search engine. It aims to make privacy policies transparant, discoverable and searchable. Various faceted search features aim to help users get novel insights into the nature of privacy policies. PrivaSeer can be used to search for privacy policy text or URLs.

PrivaSeer currently has over 1.4 million privacy policies indexed and we are always looking to add more. We crawled privacy policies based on URLs obtained from Common Crawl and the Free Company Dataset.

If you have a feature request or just want to tell us that we're doing something right, write to us on our contact page.

This research was supported in part by grants from the National Science Foundation's Secure and Trustworthy Computing program (CNS-2105745, CNS-2105734, CNS-2105736).
